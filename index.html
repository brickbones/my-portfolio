Jose Fremaint

I'm a Web Designer and Front-end Developer from Orlando, Florida. With more than 8 years of experience in the design and online industry, I’ve worked with small and large companies all over the world providing a great quality service as a Web Designer and Front-end Developer. I started as a Graphic Designer at Blue Cross Blue Shield in Puerto Rico and one year later I was working as a freelancer. While working as a freelancer I spent four years learning more about coding and online marketing.

I began my career working with small businesses; meanwhile, gaining the experience and the credibility to be able to acquire a proficient portfolio which got me the trust of medium and large companies. Currently I’m working with a local online marketing company in Orlando, Florida as a Web Designer and WordPress Front-end developer. Building something from nothing, is what I love to do and is what I do everyday. For me, every job is an opportunity to learn and grow as a professional and as a person. Technology is changing and growing exponentially, and I’m proud to be a part of a new era in technology.
